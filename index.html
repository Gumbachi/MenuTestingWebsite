First Time Learning of Website Navigation Menus
Informed Consent Agreement

Please read this consent agreement carefully before you decide to participate in the study.

Purpose of the research study

The purpose of this study is to test and see how fast people learn different web menus.

What you will do in the study

In the study, each participant will navigate through 4 different menus. For each menu, the participant will go through 10 tasks. These tasks are finding a given item in the menu. For example, if the website was for a restaurant the item the participant might need to find could be “Beverages”. Each time you are finding a different item in the menu you will be timed. To start the timer there will be a start button with the item you need to find next to it. After you have completed all 15 tasks for the current menu, you will move to the next menu. This cycle will repeat until all menus are completed. You will also be taking a survey before participating in the menu tasks. You can skip any question in the survey if it makes you uncomfortable.

Time required

The study will require about 10-20 minutes to complete.

Risks

There are no anticipated risks in this study.

Benefits

There are no direct benefits to you for participating in this research study. The study may help us understand what menus are better for web design. What menus do people navigate faster?

Confidentiality/Anonymity

The information that you give in the study will be anonymous. Your name will not be collected or linked to the data. There will be a survey that will collect your gender and your age. If you are under 18 please don't do the experiement.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation in the study is completely voluntary.

Right to withdraw from the study

You have the right to withdraw from the study at any time without penalty

How to withdraw from the study

If you want to withdraw from the study, just leave the website. There is no penalty for withdrawing.

Debriefing

Before you begin the study there will be extensive instructions on what to do.

Payment

You will receive no payment for participating in the study.
